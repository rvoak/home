I am graduate student at the School of Information at the University of California, Berkeley. My research interests lie in the areas of Machine Learning, Security and Privacy. Specifically, my recent work has focused on malware detection, social engineering defenses, adversarial machine learning, fake news detection and anomaly detection.

I have been extremely fortunate to have worked with esteemed faculty like Prof. Dawn Song and Prof. Hany Farid. In addition, I have had fruitful collaborations with Dr. Min Du and Dr. Sadia Afroz.
